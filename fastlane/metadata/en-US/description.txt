Take back control of your inbox.

Simply Filter SMS is a privacy-first, open-source text message filter for iPhone and iPad. Create keyword rules and smart filters to automatically move unwanted messages from unknown senders to Junk, Promotions, or Transactions - completely on-device.

What's new in this version:
• AI Filtering - Automatic filtering is now AI-powered, smarter and more accurate, with support for 10 languages including Spanish, French, German, Arabic, and more.
• Trusted Countries - Automatically allow messages from specific countries by phone prefix.
• 8 New Languages - The app is now available in Spanish, Arabic, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, and Italian.

In case you missed, here's what was updated recently:
• Accessibility Support - Fully usable with VoiceOver, Voice Control, Dynamic Type and Reduce Motion.
• Fresh New Look - Redesigned with the latest iOS visual style for a cleaner, modern experience.
• Native iPad Support - Full iPad experience with sidebar navigation and optimized layouts for larger screens.
• Tip Jar - Support the app's development with an optional tip. Simply Filter SMS is free forever.
• Multiple bug fixes and performance improvements.
